Cross-channel ad startup AdStage raises $2M

AdStageannouncedtoday that it has raised an additional $2 million from Verizon Ventures.

The company offers tools for businesses to manage their ad campaigns across Facebook, Google, Bing and elsewhere, and it says it’s currently managing $100 million in quarterly ad spend. CEO Sahil Jain recently told me thathis goal is to create a broader marketing platformincluding content marketing, marketing automation and sales automation, as well as ads.

Verizon Ventures previously ledAdStage’s $6.25 million Series A. (Verizon owns AOL, which owns TechCrunch.)
